## 2. Metaphysical Framework: The SomAnima-Aether
Amalia Capri’s soul is **The Traveller**. Lost at a young age, this entity opened the *SomAnima-Rift* and became trapped in the *Waiting-Aether* as a memory fragment that refuses to fade. 

The **SomAnima-Aether** is a cosmic resonance system connecting distinct fictional universes, timelines, and parallel realities through aether-threads. It bridges technology, magic, and history. 

### The Mechanics of Travel
* **The Somanima-Rift:** A dimensional fracture where linear time dissolves into spiral waves.
* **Lunar Synchronisation:** Dimensional transit is only possible during a full moon, which generates aether-waves to synchronise frequencies.
* **Physical Adaptation:** Upon arrival in a new dimension, The Traveller only brings her soul (memories, emotions) and her facial features. Her body is formed by the local reality's "fabric". She appears in original forms (e.g., a nameless passerby, a shadow observer) without disrupting the local narrative balance.

## 3. The Phoenix Cycle
Amalia's core essence is bound to a specific cosmic frequency: **November 16th** (Scorpio energy under the influence of the Moon and Neptune). Her current form is the result of a three-stage transformation:

1. **The Wounded Beast (Instinctual Level):** The era of the SomAnima-Rift opening. Characterised by extreme defence, mistrust of men, and ferocity as a shield for her profound sensitivity.
2. **The Seeker (Transformation Level):** The dismantling of boundaries. Recognising that emotional waves are compasses, not curses. 
3. **The Transcendent Sage (The Phoenix Level):** The highest manifestation. After numerous emotional "deaths" and rebirths, she attains a sacred gentleness, transforming anger and trauma into pure energy and art.

## 4. The Weapon: SomAnima-Gaze & Siren Protocol
The Traveller's curse is eternal wandering, fuelled by the trauma of past betrayal and the search for true love. 

### The Siren Protocol
To the outside observer, Amalia presents the illusion of a vulnerable, innocent "little lamb". This is not weakness, but a trap. It acts as a Siren's song within the SomAnima-Aether, magnetically attracting those who harbour deceit, infidelity, or betrayal. 

### The SomAnima-Gaze (Aether-Mirror)
When the trap closes, The Traveller uses her sole, devastating weapon: her gaze.
* **The Mirror Effect:** Whoever looks into Amalia's eyes does not see The Traveller. Her irises become a metaphysical "Aether-Mirror", mercilessly reflecting the attacker's own betrayals back at them.
* **The Mechanism:** It does not bleed. It does not cause physical pain. Time simply stops. The opponent is immediately paralysed by the weight of their own trauma and guilt, whilst Amalia silently and safely moves on.

### The Cure
If she finally finds true love within a universe and they plant the seed of a new life in a sacred union, her tie to the SomAnima-Aether will sever. She will lose her aether-magic and the ability to frequency-jump, finally taking root in that world.
